STRATEGY

Our goals were to make the website more personal, visually appealing, and informative. I was tasked with the development of the website and ensuring the final product functioned properly. As the developer, I arose to the challenge of replicating a complicated, new design and I succeeded by learning new CSS techniques, setting up the Gulp workflow, managing dependencies, and creating custom JavaScript components.

OUTCOME

The website is fully responsive across all different devices and is more user friendly. Not only is the design also more visually appealing to visitors, but it perfectly reflects our style and captures our growth as designers. We have received a lot of positive feedback about the functionality and design of this site, and we are happy with the final product.
