MELLO

STRATEGY

I was tasked to brand, design, and animate our water bottle. To begin, we conducted a lot of research to see what our demographic likes and dislikes about competing brands like Swell. We also asked them various questions on their water consumption habits. From the results, I designed a water bottle that that includes a compartment for keys and other various objects while on the go, a citrus squeezer and fruit chopper since our demographic stressed the importance of water needing to taste better, and. The water bottle also pairs with an app that reminds users to drink frequently, set goals, and interact with their peers.

OUTCOME

The water bottle has received a lot of positive feedback and successfully fulfilled all the requirements and needs our studied demographic addressed. Every aspect of the bottle itself and the app are functional and serve as motivation to consumers successfully.
