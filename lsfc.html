London Squash and Fitness Club

STRATEGY

We scheduled many meetings and location scouts with the board members of the club. Keeping in close contact with the client throughout the project enabled us to produce a site that matched their needs and wants. We attended their biggest event to photograph and capture video of the club, it’s facilities, and its members at a peak time for use on their website and social media. In addition to the website, we created a marketing plan that aimed to garner more followers and likes with techniques that include using the most popular social platforms, following similar accounts, scheduling posts to ensure they are posted consistently and at peak times, using popular hashtags, and posting tasteful content.

OUTCOME

Our redesign of the LSFC website successfully implemented all the desired features requested by our client. It used new, original photography and video to make the site more visually appealing, was better organized and included all working links so users could have a better, fully functional experience.
