Western Mustangs

STRATEGY

I produced many media guides, team recruiting booklets, team rosters, and social posts. I met with the Mustangs coach and kept in contact via email throughout the duration of the project. I used the assets he provided and I produced 2 murals that also followed the Western Mustangs brand guidelines.

OUTCOME

I gained a lot of experience producing graphics for a wide variety of stakeholders and working in a fast-paced environment. I was also able to develop a wide mix of graphic design samples too. The murals received positive feedback from the head coach and media team, and they are scheduled to be displayed in the basement of the Thompson Recreation Centre at Western University.
